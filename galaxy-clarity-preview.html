Most Profitable Service
Tree trimming at 62% margin — you're keeping 62 cents of every dollar after labor and materials. High-skill, low-material work. You're probably not charging enough for it.
Least Profitable Service
Hardscape at 28% margin. That looks like a big job — but after $460 in labor and $1,850 in materials, you're netting $890 on 16 crew-hours. If a project runs two hours long or materials overage by 10%, your margin is gone before the invoice goes out.
Pricing Adjustment Needed
Raise hardscape pricing by 12–15% or tighten scope controls. Your labor estimate is the variable — if you scope 16 hours but run 18–20, you've already given the margin away.
Bottom Line
Your maintenance and cleanup work earns more per crew-hour than your project work. Hardscape looks big — but your crews make more money mowing lawns.
